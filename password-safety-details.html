Password Safety: Building Strong Defenses

  • **Strong Passwords:** Use long, unique passphrases for every account.
  • **Avoid:** Personal information, common words, predictable patterns.
  • **Best Practice:** Use a reputable password manager to generate, store, and auto-fill complex passwords.
  • **Benefit:** Reduces risk of credential stuffing attacks.
