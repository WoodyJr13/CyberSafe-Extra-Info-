Common Red Flags

A Sense of Urgency

Scammers use phrases like "Immediate Action Required!" or "Account will be suspended in 24 hours" to rush you into making a decision without thinking.

Threatening Language

Phrases like "Account Locked," "Suspicious Activity," or "Action Required" are used to create panic and rush you into making a mistake.

Suspicious Links & Attachments

Always check the sender's email address and hover over links before clicking. Look for misspellings like "yourbankk.com" or weird URLs. Never open attachments you weren't expecting, especially from unknown senders, as they can contain malware like viruses or ransomware.

Generic Greetings

Legitimate companies will usually address you by your name. Vague greetings like "Dear Customer" or "Valued Member" are a red flag.
