About CyberSafe

CyberSafe was created by Ved Shah, a rising sophomore at David Brearley High School in New Jersey. Inspired by the NJCCIC Cybersecurity Basics program, Ved recognized the online risks faced by vulnerable groups in our increasingly technological world. This website is the result of his vision: to make cybersecurity education accessible, engaging, and practical for everyone. It aims to help users build essential skills to stay safe in the digital age.
