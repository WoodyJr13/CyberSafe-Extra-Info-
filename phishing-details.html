Phishing: In-Depth

  • Deceptive Tactics: Attackers disguise themselves as trustworthy entities (banks, government, tech support).
  • Common Channels: Emails, text messages (smishing), phone calls (vishing).
  • Goal: To trick you into revealing sensitive information (usernames, passwords, credit card details).
  • Red Flags: Sense of urgency, grammatical errors, unusual sender addresses, suspicious links/attachments.
  • Prevention: Always verify requests by navigating directly to official websites, never click suspicious links.
