Social Engineering: Understanding the Human Element

  • **Manipulation:** Exploits human psychology and error, not technical vulnerabilities.
  • **Tactics:**
    • **Pretexting:** Creating a fabricated scenario (e.g., "I'm from IT, I need your password to fix an issue").
    • **Baiting:** Offering something enticing (e.g., "free movies" link) to get credentials.
    • **Tailgating:** Following someone into a restricted area.
  • **Prevention:** Be skeptical of unexpected requests, verify identity independently, don't let urgency override caution.
